Why major in Computer and Information Sciences at Cerritos College?

CIS Related Image

CIS Programs - Degrees and Certificates of Achievement

The Computer and Information Sciences Department offers programs which prepare students to meet the challenges of the Information Technology (IT) industry as well as transfer to a four-year college or university.

The CIS programs offered at Cerritos College prepare you to work as a software developer, systems analyst, systems administrator/operator, networking technician, database administrator, and web site/page designer.


Industry Experienced Faculty

The Computer and Information Sciences professionals offer expertise in the effective and efficient use of computer systems for tackling a broad spectrum of practical challenges emerged from all the aspects of society.

The CIS curriculum provides students with broad and deep knowledge and skills on computer systems and their applications. The field of computer information systems is always evolving, and our faculty members have active and diverse research that spots trends and builds knowledge in the industry.


Growth Industry

Computer science is a dynamic and rapidly growing area that has become an integral part of the world that we live in today.
